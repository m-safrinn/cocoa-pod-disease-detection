What We Detect
Cocoa Pod Diseases

CocoaGuard detects four key cocoa pod diseases with high accuracy.

🖤
Black Pod Rot
Caused by Phytophthora fungus. Spreads rapidly in wet conditions and can devastate entire crops.
High Risk
❄️
Frosty Pod Rot
A fungal disease that covers pods in white spores. One of the most destructive cocoa diseases globally.
High Risk
🐛
Mirid (Capsid)
Insect pest damage caused by mirids feeding on pods and stems, leading to scarring and yield loss.
Medium Risk
✅
Healthy Pod
No disease detected. Your cocoa pod is in good condition. Continue regular monitoring and care.
No Risk
